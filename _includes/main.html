Higham Hill Hub is in the former bowls club in Higham Hill Park. Our facilities include a hall, small kitchen, toilets (including disabled toilet) and a meeting room. The 6m x 12m hall includes a sprung floor (6m x 7m, this area will be mirrored soon) and a cosy corner with beanbags and children’s bookshelf.

The building is wheelchair accessible and there is also a large, private lawn.

The Hub is affordable to hire for everything from classes to meetings to birthday parties.
