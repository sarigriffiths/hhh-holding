Higham Hill Hub is a Community-Interest-Company based within Higham Hill Park whose social purpose is to facilitate, host and deliver a diverse range of activities & seasonal events for all generations of the community.

Higham Hill Hub’s facilities include a hall, small kitchen, toilets (including disabled toilet), small office and a meeting room. The 6m x 12m hall includes a sprung dance and mirrors and a cosy corner with beanbags and children’s bookshelf.

The building is wheelchair accessible and we also have a large, private lawn.

The Hub is affordable to hire for everything from classes, to meetings to birthday parties.
